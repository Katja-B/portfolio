Communication & Digital Marketing

I help bridge communication gaps among people, ideas and organizations.

For over 10 years in communication, I’ve been helping people overcome language barriers, discover knowledge fostered by experts from other cultures, connect with a large international organization, perform tasks more effectively and solve business problems.

Regardless of the industry, topic or project, I use my time-tested, rhetoric-based method:

  1. Identify the audience
  2. Discover its problem
  3. Solve the problem using the right communication medium.
This applies to writing marketing copy for business executives, providing product specs to technical users, or promoting an international organization to general public. That’s how I bridge the gap between what is versus what is perceived (communicated).

And when I’m not working, I enjoy traveling and exploring new countries, learning languages (I currently speak 7), and getting lost in a good book.
