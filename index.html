About me

I help bridge communication gaps among people, ideas and organizations.

For over 10 years in communication, I've worked in digital marketing, technical writing, international relations, translation and education.

In all these roles, I’ve been helping people solve business problems, perform tasks more effectively, connect with a large international organization, discover knowledge fostered by experts from other cultures, and overcome language barriers.

Regardless of the industry, topic or project, I use my rhetoric-based 3-step process:

  1. Identify the audience
  2. Discover its problem
  3. Address the problem with the right communication medium.

This process has helped me create targeted and relevant content while writing marketing copy for business executives, providing product specs to technical users, or promoting an international organization to general public.

And when I’m not working, I enjoy traveling and exploring new countries, learning languages (I currently speak 7), and getting lost in a good book.
