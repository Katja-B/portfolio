Website projects for small businesses

TaLa

Eastern-European market

Website for a small convenience store — a hidden gem — previously promoted only by word of mouth within the Russian community in NC.

The purpose of this website was to establish the business’ online presence and attract a wider range of customers.

To build this website, I followed agile approach to

  • get customer requirements,
  • create the content from scratch (translating and localizing all product names from Russian),
  • design the logo,
  • organize information following the UX/IA best practices,
  • create a prototype,
  • review it with the customer,
  • publish the final version through Squarespace, and
  • revise the content based on customer feedback and website performance reports.

RootsGR

Digital platform for local distribution

Website for a startup that’s raising funding to grow its digital platform that connects local vendors with food retailers and distributors.

The purpose of this website was to establish the business’ online presence and communicate its concept, without giving away any trade secrets.

To create this website, I followed agile and design thinking methodologies to

  • gather information and messaging direction from the stakeholders,
  • research the market and competition,
  • follow UX/IA best practices to select the theme and imagery, write copy, and create forms, and
  • publish and update the website with GoCentral website builder.
